NOTAM Viewer

This tool parses NOTAMs and displays their geographic coordinates on an interactive map. You can copy and paste NOTAMs directly from SOFIA-Briefing or autorouter into the text field below. NOTAMs with PSN (position) coordinates are shown by default (red markers). Enabling "Show all NOTAMs" will also show all other NOTAMs using qualifier line coordinates and radius (blue markers).
